# 3D Printing
We 3D printed the case and lid to hold all components together in a compact system. The design keeps the breadboard, pumps, water reservoir, desiccant reservoir, and tubing enclosed and organized, while still allowing access for adjustments.

We adjusted the fit so everything sits securely without extra empty space, which helps keep the device compact. The lid was designed to fit tightly onto the chamber while still allowing easy access to add water or replace the desiccant beads.
